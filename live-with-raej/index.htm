Live with RaeJ & Brown Shuga Sunday Brunch

 
 

Reneé A. Johnson, a native New Yorker, is publically known as LadyRaeJ Johnson; a local Public Radio Personality at Mix 99.5 FM and TV Show Host/Executive Producer known from Japan to Switzerland, now adds to her credentials Published Author of the book, “Pretty In His Presence” which she co-authored.

Her projects and shows attributed to her production credit from radio are: Dallas during Your Drive (WGNU AM) 2011, Discussions with Dallas Live w RaeJ, The Easy Treatment Lounge and Brown Shuga Sundays Jazz Show on (Ferguson USA Hot Talk Internet Radio) from September 2014-October 2017. In addition, LadyRaeJ was formerly a Podcaster with Storm Talk 365 from 2015-2016 and has now begun her own internet radio station.

Lady RaeJ has interviewed Reverend Al Sharpton, Congresswoman Maxine Waters, controversial Umar Johnson, and celebrities such as 80’s rapper Spice 1, R&B group LeVert, Adina Howard, Michael Black of GQ, International group Maddam from Switzerland, Pauli Carmen of the group Champaign, and covered missing persons news stories and interviewed the Mayor of Ferguson, MO during the Mike Brown uprising.

About KOVL

 
 

KOVLRADIO.COM was founded by two individuals who have a passion for getting their voices heard and information to the masses. Utilizing devices such as cell phones, tablets, laptops and desktops, these two individuals want to bring you the most in entertainment media! Housed under the umbrella of the parent company of EM3 Productions, LLC, KOVLRADIO.COM will engage you with entertainment three ways; radio streaming, video and social media!

Lady RaeJ and ESmooth Weathersby started out putting the station together one mic at a time and eventually built KOVLRADIO.COM...Rulers Over The Land bringing you music that rules! Lady RaeJ in a third partner in August of 2017. Bobby Bouché became one of the partners sharing in the building of the station and was followed by Jamel “JJ The WattaBoy” Johnson in November of 2017 as the final EM3 Productions partner.

Our streaming media will present to our listeners a continuous stream of audio that typically cannot be paused or replayed, much like traditional broadcast media; in this respect, it is distinct from on-demand file serving. We are not a podcast since the broadcast does not require our listeners to download but to stream our internet broadcasted shows.
